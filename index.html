About The Event

Learn It : Bridging Academia and Industry.
The Learn It sessions bridged academia and industry, showcasing AI's transformative impact. Renowned speakers shared insights, covering AI advancements and sector implications, engaging a diverse audience eager to stay updated with latest AI trends.

Do It: Hands-on AI experience.
Do It workshops offer hands-on experience, translating theory into practice for students and professionals alike. Led by expert facilitators, attendees engage in coding exercises, data analysis tasks, and machine learning experiments with real-world datasets. These sessions foster experiential learning and innovation, preparing participants for careers in AI while enhancing skills and staying relevant in a rapidly evolving field.

Tensor Premier League: Uniting startups, professionals, students.
The Tensor Premier League (TPL) promotes innovation and collaboration among startups, professionals, and students. Participants tackle real-world AI challenges, mentored by industry experts, refining their ideas and gaining valuable insights. TPL offers a platform for students to showcase their AI skills and potentially secure career opportunities with participating startups, facilitating a direct pathway into AI careers.
